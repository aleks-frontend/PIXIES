I lead him across the street to the grain elevators. I had heard some of the kids saying that they had climbed them and I thought it would be a blast. I asked him if he had ever been on top of them and he said that he had. I asked him if he wanted to climb to the top with me and he said yes. Then I smiled at him wickedly and asked him if he wanted me to go first.
