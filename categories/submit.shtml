We now have an automated submission process. This means your stories are submitted instantly in the category of your choosing. If you would like help to edit/proof your stories then please see the bottom of the page. Please read and follow these instructions:

  1. Firstly you need to register a members account on our forums. This is completely free and only takes a few seconds. The reason for this is so that you can post all your stories under your name which makes it easy for people to find other stories you have written. It also enables you to recieve feedback from readers. Please click here to register if you have not already done so (opens in new window).

  2. Click on the appropriate category for your story below. A new window will open into which the submission process will continue.

    Story Categories: Age Gap Stories, Anal Stories, BBW Stories, Boy/Girl Stories, Dominance Stories, Erotic Novels, Exhibitionism/Voyeur Stories, Fantasy Stories, Fetish Stories, Gay Stories, Group Sex Stories, Incest Stories, Lesbian Stories, Interracial Stories, Masturbation Stories, Oral Stories

  3. In the new window type in your user name and password from <1> (1). Type in the title of your story where indicated (2). Insert main story body in the large box, either by copy and pasting, or via typing (3).

    .

  4. You can format your text, or insert pictures, using the panel above the story box. For more instructions on this click here.

  5. When you are done click "post new Story" button or "preview" to be shown a preview of your story - if it all looks good then click "post" and you're done (4). You can recieve feedback either via your email address, or via the personal message system on the forums, depending upon the settings you select in your control panel.

  6. You can edit your post, once submitted, to make any corrections/formatting. You can also add new posts to your story to continue it whilst other people cannot.

  7. Stories will be deleted if they contain unsuitable material - bestiality, rape and children to name a few. This includes persons under the age of 18!

Proof Reading/Editing of Stories:

If any authors wish to have help in writing/finishing their stories they can send it to one of our editors you will proof read and help you in any way they can. Please email your stories to storys@pixies-place.com with instructions as to how we can help!
